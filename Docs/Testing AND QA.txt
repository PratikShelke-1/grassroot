Testing & Quality Assurance 
1.Issue Identification and Prioritization:
	As a QA lead, I want to identify and prioritize testing issues to ensure we focus on the most critical aspects of the application.

2. Test Plan Development:
	As a QA lead, I want to create a comprehensive test plan that outlines the testing strategy for the entire application.
	
3. Security Testing:
	As a QA engineer, I want to conduct security testing to identify and mitigate vulnerabilities in the system.

4. Functional Testing:
	As a QA engineer, I want to execute functional tests to ensure the application performs as expected.

5. Usability and User Experience Testing:
	As a QA engineer, I want to evaluate the usability and user experience of the application.
	
6 Compatibility Testing:
	As a QA engineer, I want to perform compatibility testing to ensure the application works on various browsers and devices.
	
7.Special Award Application Testing:
	As a QA engineer, I want to validate the special award application feature to ensure it functions correctly.

8 Data Protection Compliance Testing:
	As a QA engineer, I want to verify that the application complies with data protection regulations.

9 Accessibility Testing:
	As a QA engineer, I want to assess the application's accessibility and ensure it meets WCAG standards.

10 User Support and Training Evaluation:
	As a QA lead, I want to assess user support resources and training to ensure users have the necessary guidance.
